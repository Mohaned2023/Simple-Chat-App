- Help:
    - :help  -> To get this message.

- Account:
    - :login           -> To login.
    - :register        -> To register.
    - :info            -> To get your informations.
    - :info <username> -> To get user information.
    - :delete          -> To delete your account.
    - :update          -> To update your account.

- Conversation:
    - :create <username> -> To create conversation with user.
    - :chats             -> To get your conversations.

- Chat:
    - :chat <conversation-id>  -> To enter a conversation.
